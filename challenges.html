the italian stallion challenge

Get ready for the ultimate fusion of speed and endurance with Rocky Run’s Italian Stallion Challenge—a 13.1-mile half marathon created by combining the thrill of a 5K with the endurance of a 10-mile race. This unique event is designed to push your limits and reward your Rocky spirit every step of the way, culminating in the finish line where you'll proudly claim three stunning medals. Lace up and embrace the challenge—each mile is your pathway to glory!

MOUNT
DRAGO

Drago must break you! Get ready to conquer the beast that is Mount Drago on our 10 Mile course—a towering hill where every step upward is timed with precision. Using state-of-the-art timing mats, we track your ascent from the base to the summit. The fastest 100 finishers will earn the legendary Mount Drago finisher medal—a true badge of honor for those who dare to push their limits.
